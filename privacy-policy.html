Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

At PitchTech ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect includes:

  • Personal Data: Personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details, that you voluntarily give to us when filling out our contact forms or requesting project consultations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you to:

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others.

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information.
